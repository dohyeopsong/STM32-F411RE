Scenario of single master and single slave(One to One)

Hardware of software slave select management can be set using the SSM
    bit in the SPIx_CR1 register.

Software:
    Software NSS management(SSM = 1): 
    in this configuration, slave select information is driven internally 
    by the SSI bit value in register SPIx_CR1. The external NSS pin 
    is free for other application usese.

Hardware:
    Hardware NSS management (SSM = 0):
    NSS pin must be in output mode. The NSS pin is managed by the Hardware

    NSS pin bust be pulled low to active slave to communicate with master.


Scenario of Master and three independent slaves
:
    In this application you cannot use software slave management.
    You have to use hardware slave management


Points to remember

Senario-1:
1 master and 1 slave
    1) You need not to use NSS pin of master and slave if you use software slave 
    management
    2) If you don't want to use software slave management then you can connect NSS of
    master to NSS of slave

Senario-2:
1 master and multiple slaves
    1) You connot use software slave management here
    2) You connot use NSS pin of the mster to connect to NSS pin of any of the slave.
    3) Master has to use some of its GPIO pins to control the different NSS pins of the slaves.
