If you use PLL and drice APB1 bus at 42MHz, 
then SPI2 and SPI3 can produce SCLK of 21MHz.

That means SPI peripheral's maximum data transfer rate
will be 21 million bits per second.

So, if we use the internal RC oscillator of 16Mhz as our system clock then SPI1/SPI2/SPI3 peripherals can able to produce the serial clock of maximum 8MHz.
